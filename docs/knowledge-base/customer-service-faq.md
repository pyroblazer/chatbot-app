# Customer Service Knowledge Base

## General Policies

### Return Policy
- Items can be returned within 30 days of purchase
- Items must be in original condition with tags attached
- Proof of purchase required (receipt or order number)
- Refunds processed within 5-7 business days
- Sale items may have different return windows - check product page

### Shipping Information
- Standard shipping: 3-5 business days
- Express shipping: 1-2 business days
- Free shipping on orders over $50
- International shipping available to 50+ countries (7-14 business days)
- Order tracking provided via email once shipped

### Payment Methods
- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- Buy now, pay later: Klarna, Afterpay (select items)
- Gift cards accepted

## Common Issues and Resolutions

### Order Not Received
1. Check tracking email for shipping updates
2. Verify delivery address on order confirmation
3. Check with neighbors and building management
4. If marked delivered but not received: wait 24 hours (carrier errors happen)
5. After 24 hours, contact support with order number for investigation

### Wrong Item Received
1. Take photos of what you received
2. Contact support within 7 days of delivery
3. We will arrange return shipping at no cost
4. Replacement sent within 2-3 business days
5. Or full refund processed if replacement unavailable

### Damaged Item
1. Do not discard damaged items or packaging
2. Take photos immediately upon opening
3. Report within 48 hours of delivery
4. Support will arrange free return and send replacement
5. Expedited replacement shipping for damaged goods

### Account Issues
- Password reset: Click "Forgot Password" on login page
- Account locked: Usually releases after 24 hours, or contact support
- Payment method declined: Contact your bank, card may be flagged
- Order cancellation: Possible only within 1 hour of placing order

## Customer Service Best Practices

### Tone and Communication
- **Listen first**: Let the customer fully explain before responding
- **Acknowledge feelings**: "I understand how frustrating this must be"
- **Avoid blame**: Never say "you should have" or "that's your fault"
- **Be specific**: Give exact timelines, not vague promises
- **Follow up**: Confirm the issue is resolved after solution applied

### Escalation Procedure
Escalate to supervisor when:
- Customer requests to speak to manager
- Issue involves >$500 value
- Legal threats or mentions of regulatory bodies
- Second contact about same unresolved issue
- Media or social media threats

### De-escalation Techniques
1. Stay calm - do not match customer's emotional intensity
2. Lower your speaking pace and volume
3. Use the customer's name
4. Focus on what you CAN do, not what you cannot
5. Offer choices to restore sense of control
6. "I want to make this right for you"

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do you price match?**
A: Yes, we match prices from major retailers. Contact support with a link to the lower price within 7 days of purchase.

**Q: Can I change my order after placing it?**
A: Orders can be modified within 1 hour. After that, the order may have entered fulfillment.

**Q: Do gift cards expire?**
A: Gift cards are valid for 3 years from the date of issue and have no fees.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes, we use SSL encryption and are PCI DSS compliant. We never store full card numbers.
